Introduction

My name is Steinar Oddsson and i am a 26 year old student living in Iceland. Almost all of my life i have been very interested in both video games and video game design, although never chased any sort of study to learn the aspects of that industry. Most of my career i have spent in the Aquacultural businness focusing on landbased aquaculture and fish farming. Although this was not something i had always wanted to study this was something i had the most experience in prior to my education and i had been working in that field of work since i was 13 years old. Recently i enrolled in the University of Akureyri in Iceland to learn marine biology to add to my to prior education of landbased aquaculture, after a short time i realised that this is not something i wish to study and could already feel that a career change was needed and after spending most of my free time learning the basics of coding and graphic design i came to the decision that video game design was something that i wish to chase and study. I am the type of person that when i grab an interest i get completely sucked into it and commit almost all of my free time into that interest, which is as of this moment coding and graphic design ( modding). This is also one of the main reasons i am dedicated and hopefull to start studying in that field of interest.
